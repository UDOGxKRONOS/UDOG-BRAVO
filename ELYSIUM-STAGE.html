               Points system :
               
               • 1st place - 40 points 
               • 2nd place - 30 points 
               • 3rd place - 25 points 
               • 4th place - 20 points 
               • 5th place - 15 points 
               • 6-7 places - 10 points 
               • 8-10 places - 7 points 
               • 11-15 places - 5 points 
               • 16-20 places - 2 points 
                5 points per kill
               
               
            For violation of the regulations, a system of fines is provided:
            time - minus 50 points;
            times - exclusion from the lobby for all remaining games and cancellation of points.
               
            Each player must take a screenshot after each game 
            (altogether 12 screenshots from the team, 3 from each player). 
            Screenshots must be posted within two hours after the end of the tournament by the team captain or their manager.
               
            For the lack of screenshots, a system of penalties is provided:
            screen - 3 points;
            For a complete lack of screenshots, the team loses all points.
